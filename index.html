Who I Am 

Hi! I'm someone who thrives at the intersection of creativity, curiosity, and innovation. With a deep passion for technology, lifelong learning, and the arts, I’m always exploring new ways to blend imagination and logic. Whether it’s crafting digital projects, getting lost in an art studio, or diving into the pages of a good book, I’m constantly inspired by the beauty and complexity of creative expression. 

Outside of work, I’m a proud dog parent to three amazing pups who keep life joyful and adventurous. Traveling is one of my greatest passions—I love experiencing different cultures, landscapes, and cuisines, always on the lookout for something new to discover. Whether it’s wandering through vibrant city streets, trying an unexpected new hobby, or simply curling up with a captivating novel, I’m always up for the next adventure. 

Life’s best moments come from learning, creating, and connecting, and I’m excited for every new journey ahead!
